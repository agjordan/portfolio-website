About Me

I’m Alex Jordan, a London-based software engineer. In a past life (but still in this life) I was a Product Manager who really enjoyed working with teams to create amazing products. I felt a calling to be more hands on in the creation of products and have since explored more of the coding side of product development.

I’m equally (un)comfortable in the front and back end but that is also where I like to live, just on the edge of comfort where I am still learning. I feel most confident coding in Javascript and Python but I am also familiar with a few others, namely HTML/CSS, Ruby, Lua, and C.
